What is Meta Course?

Meta Course is an educational platform that converts lecture recordings and whiteboard images into actionable study resources. Built with React and TypeScript, it uses Gemini 2.5 Flash to process audio transcriptions and visual content, then generates quizzes, flashcards, summaries, and exam preparation materials‚Äîstoring everything locally on your device.

Multimodal Processing

Gemini 2.5 Flash processes both audio transcriptions and whiteboard images to extract educational content.
